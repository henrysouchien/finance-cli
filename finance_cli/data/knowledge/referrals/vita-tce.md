# IRS VITA/TCE Free Tax Return Preparation

VITA and TCE are the IRS-sponsored free tax-preparation programs staffed by IRS-certified volunteers. They are the first routing surface for users with straightforward returns who fit current-year program scope.

Use this referral when the user needs basic return preparation, cannot afford a paid preparer, has limited English access, has a disability, or is an older taxpayer who may benefit from TCE's retirement-income familiarity. Appointments are seasonal and site-specific; the user should verify availability through the IRS locator or by phone.

This referral is not appropriate for returns outside volunteer scope, such as meaningful self-employment complexity, rentals, partnership or S-corp K-1s, multi-state complexity, or open IRS disputes.
