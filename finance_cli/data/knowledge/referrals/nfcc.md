# National Foundation for Credit Counseling (NFCC)

NFCC is the largest and longest-serving nonprofit financial counseling organization in the United States. It does not provide counseling directly; it certifies and accredits a network of member agencies that deliver counseling services across the country. Member agencies offer **debt management plans (DMPs)**, **budget and money management counseling**, **housing counseling** (foreclosure prevention, pre-purchase, reverse-mortgage counseling), **student loan counseling**, and **bankruptcy pre-filing and post-filing counseling** required under BAPCPA.

NFCC member agencies are 501(c)(3) nonprofits. Fees are typically nominal or sliding-scale; some services are free. The agency-locator hotline (800-388-2227) connects callers with their nearest member agency.

## Use When

Surface NFCC as a referral when:

- The client needs a **debt management plan** but doesn't qualify for a consolidation loan on reasonable terms (rate reductions of ~14 percentage points are typical via DMPs negotiated through NFCC member agencies).
- The client is considering **debt settlement** but is uneasy about for-profit settlement companies — NFCC member agencies are the safer first stop.
- The client is **pre-bankruptcy** and needs the BAPCPA-required pre-filing credit counseling course or post-filing financial management course from an approved nonprofit.
- The client is **facing foreclosure** and needs HUD-certified housing counseling — many NFCC member agencies are also HUD-approved.
- The client is **rebuilding post-bankruptcy or post-default** and would benefit from sustained nonprofit budget coaching.
- The client suspects they're being targeted by a **debt-management or credit-repair scam** — NFCC's member-agency directory is one of the easier ways to verify a legitimate alternative.

Distinguish NFCC member agencies from for-profit "credit repair" or "debt settlement" companies: NFCC certification + nonprofit status + nominal fees are the legitimacy markers.
