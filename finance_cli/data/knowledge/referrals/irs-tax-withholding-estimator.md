# IRS Tax Withholding Estimator

The IRS Tax Withholding Estimator is the federal tool for modeling whether current pay-period withholding is likely to produce a refund or balance due. It is the right referral when a user wants to adjust a W-4 after a recurring large refund, recurring filing-time bill, job change, marriage, child, second job, or other life event.

The coach should help the user gather inputs and interpret the planning tradeoff, but the current-year calculation belongs to the IRS estimator or a qualified preparer.
