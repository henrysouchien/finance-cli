# HUD-Approved Housing Counseling Agencies

HUD-approved housing counseling agencies provide independent counseling on homebuying readiness, rental issues, mortgage default, forbearance, foreclosure, reverse mortgages, and credit issues. The counselor works for a HUD-approved agency, not for the lender, real estate agent, or seller.

The primary locator is HUD's online housing-counselor search. Users can also call HUD at 800-569-4287 to find a nearby participating agency. The CFPB offers a parallel search tool that uses HUD-approved agency data.

## Use When

Surface this referral when:

- the user wants a human review before starting a home search or mortgage application;
- affordability math is fragile and the user needs independent pre-purchase counseling;
- the user asks about local down-payment-assistance or first-time-buyer programs;
- the user is choosing between renting and buying and wants local market counseling;
- the user is already in mortgage hardship, default, foreclosure risk, or forbearance territory;
- the user reports possible steering, discrimination, or unfair treatment in a rental or mortgage process.

Do not present a HUD-approved counselor as a lender, legal representative, real estate agent, or guarantee of program eligibility. The right framing is independent counseling and routing.
