# AnnualCreditReport.com

AnnualCreditReport.com is the federally authorized centralized site for requesting free credit reports from Equifax, Experian, and TransUnion. The same service can be reached by phone at 877-322-8228 or by mailed request form.

For homebuying readiness, this referral is a pre-application credit-review tool. It gives the user the reports that may contain errors, unfamiliar accounts, utilization patterns, or other issues to fix before a mortgage hard pull.

## Use When

Surface this referral when:

- the user is 6-12 months from a mortgage application and has not reviewed all three credit reports;
- the user does not know their credit-report contents or wants to dispute inaccurate information;
- the user reports possible identity theft, unfamiliar accounts, mixed-file issues, or incorrect late payments;
- the user's credit score is lower than expected and the next step is report review rather than score guessing;
- the user is comparing rent-vs-buy and credit readiness is one of the limiting factors.

Do not tell the user to enter personal information through a search ad, email link, or look-alike site. The safe instruction is to type the address directly or use the FTC/CFPB links to verify the official site.
