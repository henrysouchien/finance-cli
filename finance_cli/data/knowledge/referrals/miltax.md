# Military OneSource MilTax

MilTax is Military OneSource's tax resource for eligible military households. It is the right referral when military-specific facts matter: deployments, combat pay, PCS moves, multi-state complications, or eligible family-member filing support.

The coach should route the user to Military OneSource to confirm current eligibility and available support, not attempt to determine eligibility inside CashNerd.
