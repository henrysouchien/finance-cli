# Low Income Taxpayer Clinics

Low Income Taxpayer Clinics are independent organizations that may provide free or low-cost help for qualifying taxpayers with IRS disputes, audits, collection issues, appeals, or language-barrier tax-rights problems. They are not the default route for ordinary return preparation.

Use this referral when the user has an IRS notice, collection problem, unresolved prior-year issue, audit, appeal, or tax-rights problem and cannot reasonably afford representation.
